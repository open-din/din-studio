# Welcome

DIN Studio is a **desktop workspace for real-time audio patching**. You build **graphs** of **nodes** connected by **cables**, hear the result immediately, and can **export or review code** that matches what you built.

You might use it to:

- Sketch synth and effect chains visually  
- Sequence rhythms and melodies with step or piano-roll nodes  
- Shape sound with standard studio effects (dynamics, time-based FX, EQ, modulation)  
- Route signals with mixers, sends, and a single master **Output**  
- Store **samples** in the library for samplers and convolution  
- **Record** the graph master output, trim it, and send it back into your library  

The editor is organized so **browsing** stays on the left, **patching** in the center, **details and code** on the right, and **live status** along the bottom.
